SOUP RECIPIES

These Soup Recipies are very easy to make and will warm the Heart, soul and body of all ages alike cooking is always about trying new things and pushing your taste buds to the MAX.The flavor in these dishes you will enjoy all year round, take a look below at our soups and never be afraid to add a seasoning or 2 to make it your own. Enjoy and keep cooking

Chicken Noodle Soup

• 2 tablespoons butter, chicken fat or olive oil

• 2 tablespoons butter, chicken fat or olive oil

• 1 large onion, chopped

• 2 large carrots, chopped

• 2 stalks celery, chopped (optional)

• Heaped tablespoon minced garlic (4 cloves)

• 2 bay leaves

• 3 sprigs fresh thyme or use 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme

• 1 pound skinless, boneless chicken thighs (4 or 5 thighs)

• 8 cups chicken stock or broth, low sodium or use homemade stock

• 5 ounces egg noodles (or pasta of choice)

• Salt and pepper, to taste

• 1/4 cup fresh parsley, finely chopped

• Water or more stock, as needed

Lobster Bisque

• 1 pound cold water lobster tails (freshly thawed)

• 1 tablespoon olive oil

• 1 onion (diced (I use my food processor))

• 2 carrot (diced (I use my food processor))

• 1 can of Clam juice

• 1 box Chicken Broth

• 8oz Half and half

• 8oz can of Tomato paste

• 1 stick of Butter

• 8oz of Worcestershire sauce

• Old Bay Seasoning (to taste)

• Cornstarch to thickening

Beef Chili

• 1 lb. ground beef (we used 15% fat)

• 1/2 medium yellow onion (finely diced)

• 1 tablespoon minced garlic

• 1 15-oz. can pinto beans (drained and rinsed)

• 1 15-oz. can kidney beans (drained and rinsed)
